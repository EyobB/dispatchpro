Introduction

SleekHaul ("we," "our," or "us") operates the SleekHaul Tracking mobile application (the "Service") and the website sleekhaul.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, company information
  • Device Information: Device ID, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, feature interactions, crash reports

Location Information

Important: Our Service is designed to track location for delivery and logistics purposes.
  • Real-time GPS coordinates: Collected when tracking is active
  • Location history: Stored for route optimization and reporting
  • Background location: Collected when the app is running in the background (with your permission)

Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Time zone setting
  • Operating system and platform
  • Device identifiers

Primary Services

  • Reporting: Generate delivery reports and analytics
  • Fleet Management: Enable fleet managers to monitor driver locations

Service Improvement

Communication

Business Operations

Legal Requirements

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular Audits: Security practices are regularly reviewed and updated
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hosted on secure, industry-standard platforms

Location Data Specific Protections

Given the sensitive nature of location data:

  • Location data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Access to location data is restricted to authorized users only
  • Location data retention periods are clearly defined
  • Users can disable location tracking at any time through app settings

Your Privacy Rights

Location Control
